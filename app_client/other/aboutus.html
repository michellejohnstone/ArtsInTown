What is Arts in Town?

Arts in Town was created for users to find and crowdsource local art, cultural and music events in the Bay Area. Users can learn about local events by reviewing our events page. Each event is linked to a specific page that provides information about location and details. Users can also leave comments about events. The comment thread is available for attendees to discuss the event and sell tickets. Register and login to save events you want to attend or create your own.
